It's designed to ensure you are informed about what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and how you can manage and control your data. Some terms used in this Privacy Policy may have specific meanings as defined in the User Agreement and should be interpreted consistently.

1. Information We Receive About You

When we refer to "Information," we mean any data related to you that may include, but is not limited to, personal data.

YOURS collects information in two primary ways:

  • Information you provide directly to us.
  • Information collected automatically through your use of our Services.

1.1 Data You Provide to Us

Account Creation Data:

This is data you provide when creating an account, which includes:

  • Required: Phone number, name, username, date of birth, and your favorite movies, books, series and albums.
  • Optional: Profile picture and biography.

Content Data:

We receive content you create or share through our platform, including:

  • Posts, reactions, comments, and any additional data you choose to share on your profile (such as favorite movies, books, etc.).

Communications with YOURS:

Any messages, support requests, or other interactions you have with us, which may include:

  • The content of emails, support tickets, or requests for information, as well as any other personal identifiers you provide.

1.2 Data We Collect Automatically When You Use Our Services

As you use our Services, we automatically receive certain data from your device and your interaction with YOURS. This includes:

Connection and Technical Data:

  • IP address, account creation date, last login, terminal equipment, device and operating system details, system configuration, and mobile advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA for iOS or AAID for Android).

Interaction Data:

Data about your social interactions and how you engage with others on the platform, such as:

  • Friends, reactions to posts, comments, number of friends, and interaction frequency.

Usage Data:

This includes data related to your activity on the platform, such as:

  • Time of posting, post views, content engagement (likes, comments), and actions like hiding or blocking users.

Device and Equipment Data:

Information about the type of device and system you use, including:

  • Device model, operating system version, language preferences, and any application crashes or performance data.

2. How We Use Information We Receive About You

We do not sell your information to anyone. We carefully manage your data to provide and improve our services. Here's how we use your information:

  • Operating and Improving Our Services: To create your account, display content, and improve functionality based on your usage. This includes personalizing your experience based on your tastes and preferences.
  • Safety and Security: To maintain the security and integrity of the platform, ensure compliance with laws, and protect users from fraudulent activity or harm.
  • Advertising: While we currently do not serve ads, if we do in the future, we may use your data to tailor advertisements to your preferences.
  • Analytics and Performance Measurement: We analyze user behavior to improve app performance, stability, and to provide personalized content recommendations.
  • Communications: To send you updates, respond to your inquiries, and notify you about features or important updates to our Services.
  • Research and Development: Occasionally, we may invite you to participate in research or surveys to improve YOURS. Participation is always voluntary.

3. When We Share Information About You

We only share your information when necessary to provide our Services, protect our platform, or comply with legal obligations. This typically falls into the following categories:

  • YOURS Employees: Authorized employees may access your information to provide support or ensure service operation.
  • Other Users: Content you share may be visible to your friends or other users, depending on your privacy settings.
  • Service Providers: We work with third-party partners who assist with various services, such as hosting, analytics, and security. We share the minimum necessary data with these providers.
  • Ad Partners and Analytics Companies: If we begin serving ads, our partners may use their own tracking technologies (such as SDKs) to help tailor ads to users' interests. We will provide a list of such partners and their privacy policies.
  • Partner Integrations: If you use features that require partner integrations (e.g., API links to third-party platforms), we may share relevant data to enable the integration.
  • Legal and Compliance: We may share data if required by law or to protect the safety of users, respond to legal requests, prevent fraud, or address security concerns.
  • Change in Ownership: If YOURS undergoes a business change such as a merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. How Long We Keep Information About You

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our Services or comply with legal obligations. Below are the general retention timelines:

  • Account Creation Data: Retained as long as your account is active. Deleted upon account deletion, except as required for legal purposes.
  • Content Data: Retained for as long as you choose to keep it or until you delete it.
  • Communications: Retained for a maximum of 24 months.
  • Connection and Usage Data: Retained for up to 24 months unless part of account data that is kept longer.

5. Your Controls Over Your Information

You have several controls over the information you provide and how it's used:

  • Notification Settings: You can manage your notification preferences within the app.
  • Privacy Settings: You can choose to make your profile public or "friends only" and block or hide users as needed.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time through the app. After initiating deletion, your account will be removed within 15 days unless you log in again during that period.

6. Your Rights Over Your Information

Regardless of where you live, you have rights related to your personal data, including:

  • Delete Your Account: You can delete your account at any time through the app.
  • Access Your Data: You can request a copy of your personal information by contacting our support team.
  • Correct Your Information: If your information is inaccurate, you can correct it in the app or by contacting support.
  • Appeal Decisions: You may appeal any decision related to your data by contacting our privacy support team.

7. Children's Data

YOURS does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are under 13, do not use OUR Services. If you are between 13 and 18, parental consent may be required depending on your location.

8. International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred outside the European Union or another region with strict data protection laws, we ensure it is protected under applicable legal frameworks, such as standard contractual clauses.
